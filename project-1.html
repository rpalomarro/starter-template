The Movement

BRANDING / ART DIRECTION


Young people have a lot on their plate. Pandemic. Political unrest. Precarious future for the planet. Where do you start to make positive change? The Movement is a place and program where young people can engage when they’re not sure where to start or how to help. It provides cool opportunities to volunteer at the community level and work to create change close to home.
